Investigation Overview¶

The goal of this presention is to decribe information about Fordgobike data like the most generation and the most gender use the bike of Fordgobike the average distance and duration of the trips through the weekdays and whether the the gender ,user type or the bike sharing could affect on the distance and duration of the trip.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset contains information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.
